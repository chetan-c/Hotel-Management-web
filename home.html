Room Service

Room service is a premium hospitality offering that allows guests to enjoy food, beverages,
and other amenities in the comfort of their rooms. Available 24/7 in many hotels,
this service provides a convenient and private dining experience without the need to visit the hotel's restaurant.
Guests can order meals, snacks, drinks,
and even customized dishes from a dedicated menu, which is delivered directly to their rooms by hotel staff.

Wi-Fi & Internet Facility

Wi-Fi and internet services are essential amenities provided by hotels to ensure seamless connectivity for guests.
Most hotels offer complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi in rooms, lobbies,
and common areas, allowing guests to browse the internet, stream content,
and stay connected with work or social media. Business travelers benefit from stable internet access for virtual meetings,
emails,and remote work, while leisure guests can enjoy entertainment and online services.
